Club legends

Relive the magic of club legends

Thierry Henry

A dazzling forward and Arsenal's all-time leading scorer, Thierry Henry combined blistering pace with sublime technique. He was the face of Arsenal's "Invincibles" season in 2003–04.

Iconic stat: 228 goals in 377 appearances for Arsenal.

Patrick Vieira

The midfield powerhouse of the late 90s and early 2000s, Vieira combined physical dominance with technical class. As club captain, he led the Invincibles and set the tone competitiveness.

“I loved the battles, especially against Manchester United.”

Tony Adams

A commanding centre-back and born leader, Tony Adams spent his entire career at Arsenal, captaining the side across three decades.

Iconic stat: Captained Arsenal to league titles in 3 different decades (80s, 90s, 2000s).

Dennis Bergkamp

Known as “The Iceman,” Bergkamp brought elegance and intelligence to Arsenal’s attack. His vision, first touch, and creativity defined Arsène Wenger's early success.

"I really like to create – to make goals. It’s more than just scoring."
